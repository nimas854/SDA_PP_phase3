About Me





















HOBBY

I love diving into the details of how different computers function, from desktops to laptops and even the intricate workings of mobile devices. Each system has its own unique features, and I enjoy comparing their performance, capabilities, and innovations. Whether it's learning about processors, exploring the latest smartphone releases, or understanding how operating systems manage tasks, technology never ceases to captivate me.

Exploring new websites is also something that excites me. I like examining their design, usability, and functionality, wondering how the backend is structured to deliver a seamless experience. This interest in both hardware and software fuels my passion for technology and pushes me to stay updated with the latest trends. My love for exploring different aspects of technology motivates me to continuously learn and grow within the field of software engineering.

















My Experience

My journey with technology started back in middle school when I was first introduced to basic coding. Learning to code sparked my interest in computers, and I quickly realized how much I enjoyed solving problems through programming. By the time I reached high school, I had developed a stronger understanding of coding and began applying it in practical ways, such as creating small programs and understanding the logic behind different applications.

Beyond coding, I found myself fascinated with troubleshooting issues related to phones and laptops. Over the years, I’ve gained experience fixing basic settings on laptops, solving software glitches, and even unlocking phones that had been accidentally locked. I became the go-to person among my friends and family for resolving tech-related issues. From setting up devices and adjusting system settings to downloading and managing files, I took on each challenge as a learning opportunity.
